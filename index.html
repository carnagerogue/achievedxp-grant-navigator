Compliance · Standards

Built to the standards corrections auditors already enforce.

AchieveDXP operates inside the federal and state compliance envelope agencies already live in. No exceptions carved out. No compensating controls negotiated.

CIPA
Children's Internet Protection Act
Required filtering and internet-access controls for any K–12 or juvenile-facility learning environment.
HIPAA
Health Information Privacy
Protected handling of health-adjacent learner data — SUD program enrollment, reentry case files, medical education records.
FERPA
Educational Records Privacy
Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act compliance for student transcripts, credentials, and transfer records.
SOC 2
Type II · Annual audit
Independent attestation of security, availability, and confidentiality controls — the audit your CIO already asks about.
ISO 27001
Information Security Management
International standard for information security management systems — required for most federal procurement paths.
NIST SP 800-53
Federal security controls
Federal information system security and privacy controls — the baseline DOJ, ED, and HHS grant-funded systems must meet.
